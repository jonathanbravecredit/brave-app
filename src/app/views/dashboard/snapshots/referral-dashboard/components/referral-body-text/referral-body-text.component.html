How to Earn

Get $3 for every friend you refer who doesn’t have a Brave Credit account, and who successfully checks their credit scores here. There’s a 10 person referral limit a month.

Plus, get an additional $20 a month (for a total of $50) if 10 friends successfully enroll!

The 10 person limit resets every month!

When Is it Paid Out?

If you successfully refer 10 people, your $50 gift card will be sent to your email the Monday after the 10th person enrolls.

If you have under 10 people referred for a month, you’ll get the payout the first Monday of the following month.

What Kind of Giftcard is It?

It is an Amazon gift card, which will be sent to the email you registered with.
