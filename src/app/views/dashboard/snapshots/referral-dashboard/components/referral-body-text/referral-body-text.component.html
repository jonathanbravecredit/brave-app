The 10 person limit resets every month, and no referrals roll over to new campaigns. See the full terms here.

When Is it Paid Out?

If you succesfully refer 10 people, your $35 gift card will be sent to the email on record the Tuesday after the full week the 10th person enrolls.

If you have under 10 people referred for a month, you’ll get the payout the first Tuesday of the following month.

Our partner will send Amazon gift cards, but if that is unavailable they will provide a selection of cards from a similar retailer (Walmart, ebay, or other major retailers), or a Visa card.
